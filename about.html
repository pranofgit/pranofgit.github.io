About Me

I am a theoretical physicist by training currently working as a Data scientist. Interested in learning and exploring about anything that can be expressed as numbers or equations.

I am a data scientist by profession. With a strong background in theoretical physics, I've completed my five year Integrated MSc. from IIT-Roorkee and PhD from HBNI-Mumbai specializing in string theory. Now, I find myself delving into the world of data and numbers.

Currently, I am working on analysing time series data for supply chain management. My work focuses on understanding trends and making predictions to optimize various aspects of supply chain processes.

Apart from this, I have a keen interest in quantitative finance. I believe that my interest in numbers and equations, fostered by my physics background, complements my curiosity in the financial domain.

I look forward to learning, exploring, and delving deeper into anything that can be expressed as numbers or equations.
